# Revival
Revival is a text-based social platform where users can make blog posts, follow their friends, and discover new posts in an exploration-first presentation intended to recapture the magic of the blog era internet.

## Browse Posts
- View a simple chronological list of posts from followed users
- Discover the most recent posts globally to find new favorites
- Both feeds can be filtered by tag to narrow down post topics
- Click any post to see its full contents or its author's profile
- Posts can also be upvoted or downvoted
## Explore the World, or Your Own City
- The Map page allows users to explore posts in a nonlinear, enjoyable way
- Each post can be tagged with a real-world location
- This is completely optional and can be anywhere, regardless of current location
- Easily discover restaurant reviews, hiking trails, local cafes, and more
## Craft Your Prescence
- Users can log in or create a new account with Google OAuth
- Create new posts to display on your profile alongside your name and bio
- Follow other users to never miss a new blog post
